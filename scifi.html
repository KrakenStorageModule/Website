Science Fiction

My reviews and recommendations. All opinions are unequivocably mine.

Books

Leviathan Wakes – Frank Herbert
James S.A Corey · 2011
★★★★★
My favorite modern sci-fi book. Great characters, solid writing, and absolutely fantastic world-building. As an engineer, I appreciate the hard sci-fi (science fiction that tries to be as realistic as possible) elements of the setting.
Modern Favorite Action

Movies

Alien
Ridley Scott · 1974
★★★★★
The first time I watched this was in my freshman year dorm with my friends. It seemed so absurd that I, being obsessed with science fiction, had never watched it. I'm glad I did. It was scary, it was campy, and it was fun!
Classic Horror Favorite

Shows

The Clone Wars
Cartoon Network · 2008-2013
★★★★★
Me and my dad started watching this the summer after I graduated high school. It was an easy casual watch at night after both of us had been busy all day. Since it is a children's show, a lot of the lack of nuance in characterization can be forgiven. Overall, a very fun show if you can turn your brain off.
Cartoon Lighthearted Father-Approved
